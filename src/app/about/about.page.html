Cyber Crime Reporter and News:

Your one-stop solution for reporting cyber crimes and accessing related emergency lines in Nigeria. Our mission is to create a safer and more secure community for everyone.

About the App:

This Cyber Crime Reporting and News App is a powerful tool designed to help individuals report cyber crimes quickly and efficiently while bringing news to the hands of users. In such times of unrest in the country and world at large, cyber crimes have become a significant concern, and this app aims to provide a secure and accessible platform for reporting such incidents. This App also carries tips for awareness in relation to cyber crimes, and emergency lines/e-mails for contacting crime agencies in Nigeria.

Key Features:

  • Provides a means of reporting cyber crime via Text and Multimedia to Agencies Emails.
  • Provides list of functional phone numbers of crime commision agencies.
  • Provides Cyber Crime Prevention Tips to educate users on cyber crime and help them understand how to avoid being victims.
  • Gives news on cyber crime related matters.
  • Allow for offline view of saved news and reports.
