Cyber Crime Reporter and News:

Your one-stop solution for reporting crimes and accessing related emergency lines in Nigeria. Our mission is to create a safer and more secure cyber community for everyone.

About the App:

This Cyber Crime Reporting and News App is a powerful tool designed to help individuals report cyber crimes quickly and efficiently while bringing news to the hands of users. In an increasingly digital world, cyber crimes have become a significant concern, and this app aims to provide a secure and accessible platform for reporting such incidents. This App also carries tips for awareness in relation to cyber crimes, and emergency lines/e-mails for contacting cyber crime agencies in Nigeria.

Key Features:

  • Provides a means of reporting cyber crime via Text and Multimedia to Agencies Emails.
  • Provides list of functional phone numbers of cyber crime commision agencies.
  • Provides Crime Prevention Tips to educate users on cyber crime and help them understand how to avoid being victims.
  • Gives news on cyber crime related matters.
  • Allow for offline view of saved news and reports.
